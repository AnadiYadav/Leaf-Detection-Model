About Us

Plant Classification And Disease Detection

Accurate plant disease diagnosis is crucial for global plant health. Early detection prevents problems and boosts productivity. Detecting diseases early on is paramount for preventing widespread damage and optimizing productivity in agriculture. However, relying solely on human observation for disease identification is limited and inefficient.


To address this challenge, automated systems leveraging deep learning and computer vision have emerged as indispensable tools. These systems analyse visual cues and plant characteristics to swiftly and accurately identify diseases.


This can be deployed in agricultural fields so that the whole pipeline can be automated. Our ongoing efforts are focused on refining and advancing these automated plant disease classification systems, contributing to a more resilient and sustainable landscape.
